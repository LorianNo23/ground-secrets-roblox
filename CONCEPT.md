# Treasure Hunter / Ground Secrets Concept

## Game Summary

Treasure Hunter, also called Ground Secrets, is a cozy Roblox progression game about scanning the ground, digging up hidden objects, cleaning or identifying finds, completing a collection log, selling duplicates, and upgrading equipment.

The player starts on a beach with a cheap metal detector. Better detectors, luck upgrades, and map unlocks let the player discover rarer objects in more unusual locations.

## Core Loop

1. Equip a detector.
2. Walk around a map and scan the ground.
3. Watch signal strength rise near hidden treasure spots.
4. Dig when the signal is strong enough.
5. Find an item with a rarity and value.
6. Add the item to inventory and collection progress.
7. Sell duplicates for coins.
8. Buy detector range, luck, and future area upgrades.
9. Unlock new maps with better treasure pools.

## First Milestone

Build a minimal playable beach prototype:

- Player has a basic metal detector.
- Beach map contains hidden treasure spots.
- Client shows signal strength when near a treasure.
- Player can press a key or click to scan.
- If close enough, a simple digging action finds an item.
- Item is added to a server-owned inventory table.
- Basic UI shows found item, rarity, and money.
- Player can sell found items for coins.
- Player can upgrade detector range or luck.

## Design Pillars

- Cozy and readable before flashy.
- Short feedback loops.
- Clear progression.
- Simple systems that can be expanded.
- Server-authoritative rewards, coins, inventory, and upgrades.
- Client handles input, UI, camera feel, and local feedback.
